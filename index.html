About US

South Africa’s Preferred

Solar Energy and Water Harvesting Solution Provider

Established in response to SA's resource constraints and risk across energy and water infrastructure sustainability in support of SA’s economy.
Off The Grids partners with multiple specialist companies to successfully render Green Products and Service offerings to Clients.
Client Service excellence is at the heart of our Residential, Commercial and Industrial delivery and end to end Client experience, from inception of lead to support and maintenance.
We recognise the need for Clients to simplify their Green engagements, as electrical, solar and water solutions are highly integrated.
Our Greenpreneur Partners
Teaming up with like-minded businesses who share our values is key in providing our customers the best possible service and solutions.
Meet the Leadership Team

Philip and Cathy Crous have a shared passion for sustainable living and so, Off The Grids, was born.

Philip, a seasoned Electronic and Computer Science Engineer Engineer and Cathy, an Industrial Electronic Engineer and former Financial Services Executive in Technology and Business, both offer more than 69 years of extensive experience in Energy (Eskom and renewables), ICT technologies, Digital, Electronics and the Financial Sector.
